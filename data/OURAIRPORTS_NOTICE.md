# OurAirports data notice

`airports.json` is a reduced derivative of the OurAirports `airports.csv` and `countries.csv` datasets. It contains scheduled-service airports with three-letter IATA codes and was generated on 2026-07-15.

Source: https://ourairports.com/data/

OurAirports releases its data to the Public Domain and provides it without a guarantee of accuracy or fitness for use.
